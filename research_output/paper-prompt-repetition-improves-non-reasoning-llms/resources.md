## Related Papers

### Papers This Work Builds On

| Paper | Relevance |
|-------|-----------|
| Wei et al. (2023) — Chain of Thought Prompting | Foundational reasoning technique; prompt repetition is positioned as an alternative |
| Kojima et al. (2023) — "Let's think step by step" | Seminal CoT variant; reasoning models' implicit repetition motivated this work |
| Shaier (2024) — Question repetition only | Found no gains repeating only the question (not full prompt); motivates full repetition |
| Springer et al. (2024) — Repetition in text embeddings | Parallel finding: repetition helps in embedding domain |
| Xu et al. (2024) — Re-reading improves reasoning | Related concept: re-reading as a reasoning-time strategy |
